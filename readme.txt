-----------------------------------------------------------------------
-------------------  FROGGER- Mode 3  ---------------------------------
------------------- by Tanmay Solanki ---------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

There are four areas seperated by color, land1, road, land2, water.
In the road area there is a red car moving, avoid hitting the car to
avoid losing a life. Avoid toughing the blue water as well unless on
a log or lilypad

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Objective ----------------------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

The Objective of the game is to make it to one of the three lilypads 
without dying. you do so using the controls to move up, dow, left, 
and right.

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
2. Controls -----------------------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

Up - Up Arrow
Down - Down Arrow
Left - left Arrow
Right - Right Arrow

-----------------------------------------------------------------------
3. Losing -------------------------------------------------------------
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

You lose the game by losing all three lives. You do so by hitting the
car or going into the water.
